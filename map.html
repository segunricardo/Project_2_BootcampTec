Space Map


The following map was compiled with data from the SATCAT y BOXSCORE, which we took from www.space-track.org. We also employed a GeoJson to draw the borderlines per each country. The maps shows all of the nations which have launched vehicles or cargo into space, with varying degrees of color to illustrate the intensity of the activity in space for each country. The map is able to filter and show different items in space depending on their nature (rocket body, payload, debris, and others). The data can be viewed per each 10 years of the space race and it was elaborated through a mix of Javascript and HTML element.
